## Oura
- Primary domain: https://ouraring.com
- Secondary (redirects): https://oura.com
- Current products: Oura Ring 4 (latest), Oura Ring Gen 3 (still sold)
- Subscription: Yes — Oura Membership required for most insights (~$5.99/month)
- Affiliate options: Direct brand program via Impact. Amazon Associates also viable for select SKUs.
- Affiliate URL placeholder: `{{YOUR_OURA_AFFILIATE_LINK}}`
- Notable: FDA-registered for some features but NOT FDA-approved as a contraceptive device. Natural Cycles (separate app) is the FDA-cleared cycle-tracking solution that integrates with Oura temperature data.

## Circular
- Primary domain: https://www.circular.xyz
- Shop subdomain: https://shop.circular.xyz
- Current product: Circular Ring 2 (replaces the original Circular / Circular Slim)
- Subscription: No — "subscription-free" is the brand's primary marketing wedge against Oura
- Price: ~$349–$549 depending on finish (matte black lower, gold/rose gold higher). HSA/FSA eligible. Confirm current pricing on shop page before publishing.
- Battery life: Manufacturer claim commonly cited at 5–8 days, but not always verified on a clean first-party source. Write as "battery life per manufacturer — verify on current product page" rather than a specific number.
- Distinctive: Built-in ECG with AFib detection. "Kira" in-app AI health coach. Marketing emphasizes 140+ biosignals and 13+ health features.
- Affiliate options: Awin network program (brand-direct via Awin).
- Affiliate URL placeholder: `{{YOUR_CIRCULAR_AWIN_LINK}}` — if you don't yet have an approved tracking link, use a `[AFFILIATE LINK PENDING: Circular]` placeholder linked to the plain circular.xyz product page, and flag it in the batch report. Do not fabricate or guess a tracking ID.
- **Reputational note:** Circular has accumulated a non-trivial set of Trustpilot and Kickstarter complaints regarding delivery delays and hardware quality on the first-gen product. When recommending Circular in a post, mention this context honestly rather than glossing over it. Do not compare it as a peer to Oura/Ultrahuman without noting the delivery-history caveat. Known lookalike / confusable domains to avoid linking to: circul.com, circulring.com, circularring.com, circular-ring.com, circulsense.com (see fake-domains.md).

## BKWAT
- Primary domain: https://bkwat.com
- Current products: BKWAT VDR (health tracker), BKWAT VFR (frosted finish, marketed to women and men), BKWAT RMP (couples, blood pressure emphasis). Often listed on Amazon simply as "BKWAT Smart Ring."
- Subscription: No — "no subscription" is a headline feature.
- Price: ~$45–$88 across SKUs. Budget tier — a fraction of Oura/Ultrahuman/Circular pricing.
- Battery life: Site claims up to 10 days with magnetic fast charging; Amazon listings cite 5–7 days (up to 30 days with the charging case). Write as "5–10 days (manufacturer claims vary)" rather than picking one number.
- Distinctive: IP68 water/dust resistance. 100+ sports modes, HR/BP/SpO2/sleep sensors. Positioned squarely as the affordable no-subscription alternative. Some SKUs specifically marketed to women, some to couples.
- Affiliate options: **Both Awin and Amazon programs are typically available.** When both are available, every post mentioning BKWAT should include BOTH links so readers can pick their preferred retailer:
  - **Awin (BKWAT's official brand site) placeholder:** `{{YOUR_BKWAT_AWIN_LINK}}` — use this anywhere you'd link to bkwat.com or the BKWAT product page.
  - **Amazon Associates placeholder:** `{{YOUR_BKWAT_AMAZON_LINK}}` — use this anywhere you'd link to BKWAT on Amazon.
  Use both links verbatim once filled in — do not modify either tracking code or substitute a different URL. Follow the convention of "Buy BKWAT: Amazon | Official Site" or equivalent so both links surface naturally in product-recommendation sections. If a post only links once to BKWAT (e.g., a passing mention rather than a product recommendation), default to the Awin link, since it points at the brand's own product page with full context.
- **Reputational note:** BKWAT has the hallmarks of a white-label Shopify brand running on Chinese OEM hardware (multiple SKUs, aggressive pricing, minimal public product roadmap). When reviewing it, treat on-site reviews cautiously and cite independent reviews or Amazon verified-purchase reviews where possible. Not a peer to Oura in terms of sensor validation — be specific about what it can and can't do rather than making broad accuracy claims.

## Affiliate link formatting

This skill assumes no Pretty Links plugin or other link-rewriting wrapper is in use. Tracked affiliate URLs are dropped directly into posts in their final form. Common networks and how their tracked URLs typically look:

- **Amazon Associates** — typically `amzn.to/...` short links provided per-brand. Use the URL exactly as listed in the per-brand entry above.
- **Direct brand programs** — brand-provided tracked URLs (ShareASale, Impact, brand-native), listed per-brand above.
- **Awin network programs** — typically use the `tidd.ly/...` shortener (Awin's official tracking-link domain — that's correct, not a lookalike).

Always verify each tracking URL works by clicking through in the browser before publishing. If a brand's affiliate link in this file is still a `{{YOUR_*}}` placeholder, do not publish — either fill it in with your real link or use a `[AFFILIATE LINK PENDING]` marker and flag it in the batch report so it can be swapped in later.
